# Landing Pages — Conversion Pages for Coaches

## The one-liner

Fast, conversion-built landing pages for coaches — with the form wired straight
into their CRM so no lead ever leaks.

## The core insight (the whole pitch)

**A landing page with no CRM is a leak.** Leads hit the page, fill the form, and
vanish into an inbox nobody checks. The page *looks* like the deliverable. The
thing that actually makes them money is the form connected to the CRM — captured,
tagged, followed up automatically.

The page is the hook. The CRM connection is the recurring revenue.

## Why we win

- **Speed + system.** Built with the impeccable + design skill pipeline. Days, not
  weeks. AI tells stripped out, contrast/hierarchy/motion done properly.
- **We already run GHL.** No new infra. We build the page, GHL captures the lead.
  We're connecting two things we already own.
- **Most coaches are stuck** on a Canva/Wix/half-built site with a dead form.

2. "Quick thing — where do the leads from this go right now?" (They won't have a

3. "Right now every person who fills this in lands in an inbox and gets forgotten.
